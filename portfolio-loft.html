Open volumes, without losing warmth.

Concept Statement

The thinking behind the room.

A double-height loft study exploring how a single open volume can hold three functions — living, dining and a mezzanine lounge — without losing intimacy. A dark timber stair anchors the plan and separates the ground-floor living area from the kitchen and dining zone beyond it, while a full glazed wall lets the city skyline read as part of the room's material palette after dark.
