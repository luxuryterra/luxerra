Concept I

Marble & Warm Oak

This concept pairs Calacatta marble cladding with slatted warm oak panels, creating a dialogue between cool mineral surfaces and natural timber warmth. The floating vanity in rich walnut anchors the space while a pair of oval mirrors frame the double sink with quiet symmetry.

Recessed warm lighting traces the ceiling and shelving niches, dissolving any sense of harshness — the light here is something you feel rather than see.
