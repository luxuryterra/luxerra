About Luxerra

Niusha Meymandinejad

Interior Designer · 3D Visualizer · Toronto Metropolitan University

Design is more than a discipline — it is a mindset grounded in creativity, problem-solving, and meaningful expression. An Interior Design student at Toronto Metropolitan University, Niusha approaches every project as an opportunity to create spaces that are functional, inspiring, and deeply personal.

With two years of hands-on experience in the retail design industry, she has collaborated with designers and builders to develop custom solutions, source materials, and produce high-quality 3D visualizations that communicate ideas with clarity and conviction.
