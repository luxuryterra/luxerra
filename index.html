The Studio

A quiet obsession
with how rooms feel.

We are a design studio working across restaurants, private kitchens, bathrooms and lofts — shaping space through light, material honesty, and proportion, rather than trend. Every project begins with a single question: how should this room feel to stand in?

How We Work

Three principles, in every room we touch.

01

Light

We design the path of daylight before we choose a single finish — orienting rooms so light becomes part of the material palette.

02

Material

Travertine, bronze, plaster, oak. We use few materials, chosen honestly, and let each one age the way it was meant to.

03

Proportion

Ceiling height, sightlines, the width of a doorway — proportion is the part of a room you feel before you can name it.
